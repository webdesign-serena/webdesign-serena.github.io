My hobbies, passions, and interests

Jenn Im

Jenn is a YouTuber and fashion designer and she is one of my biggest inspirations. She has helped me embrace who I am and gave me the confidence to experiment with different fashion styles. She also has motivated me to continue to challenge myself academically.

Greece

Since I was a kid, I have always enjoyed learning about Greek history and mythology. Traveling to Greece is on my bucketlist and I have always been entranced by their architecture and art. There are also islands where no cars are allowed, so you are always in for a new adventure wherever you look!

Studio Ghibili

I have watched every Studio Ghibili film ever made and they never disappoint. The films are created for a wide-range of audiences with meaningful plots that anyone can relate to. Studio Ghibili has also set the standard for Japanese animation films and is recognized worldwide for their traditional techniques.

Thrifting

During my free time, I like to venture off to my local thrift stores and find some new items that I can DIY and customize to fit the current fashion trends or my own personal style. I am always amazed by my unique finds because most of the items are truly one-of-a-kind.

Kayaking

One of my favorite activites is to go kayaking. I recently went on a kayaking tour around Sausalito and it was an amazing experience! I enjoy being surrounded by the water because it calms my mind and allows me to appreciate the scenery and escape the city life.

Learning Languages

Besides English, I can speak Cantonese, Mandarin, and some French. I know a few phrases in Korean and Japanese but I would love to learn the two languages fully. Every language is different and I find it beautiful that there are different ways to read, write, and pronounce words but they all carry the same universal meaning.

Web Designing

I was never the most artistic person but web desiging allowed me to express my creativity through a different medium. Although I only recently learned the basics of web desiging, I am impressed with the wide range of creative freedom and design I have. I intend to further myy skills and hopefully design my own fashion blog in the future so stay tune!
